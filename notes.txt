Lifecycle of Component
The series of events that happen from the mounting of the component to its Unmounting.
Mounting - Birth of Component
Update  - Growth of component
Unmounting - Death of Component


render()
It used to render HTML of the component in react. This method is required for a class based
component to render the DOM. It runs during the mounting and updating your component. It should 
be pure i.e, you can't modify states inside it.
